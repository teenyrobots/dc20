EXHALE

by hinahina gray





“Death in Hawai'i: The Epidemics of 1848-1849” by Schmitt and Nordyke published in Hawaiian Journal of History (2001).

A low estimate of a quarter of the Native Hawaiian population was decimated by a series of epidemics due to introduced diseases.

It affected kupuna - our elders, and keiki - our children, the most.
